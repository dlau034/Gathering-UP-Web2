About Us

GatheringUp makes finding and organizing events simple. If you are like most people, the process of bringing your friends together will involve finding a venue, confirming with friends, then confirming a date. In between the search and confirmation of the venue and date, there will be lots of emails/SMS messages floating around. As the number of people included in the event grows, the amount of communication and complexity will grow exponentially. In the end, the time spent in organizing the event may be more than the time spent in the event.

GatheringUp was created to lessen the pain of getting busy people together. Find something to do here. Save it to the Datebook and send it to all your friends. Once they join, you can discuss the event in the Datebook.
