Step A

1. Start building your Perfect Day by filling in the placeholder textboxes in the module on the right.

2. You can choose the categories that your Perfect Day belongs to.

3. Upload a Featured Image. The Featured Image will be displayed on the featured page with the description.

Step B

4. Your story is split up into sections. You can add a Subtitle, Text and a Picture to each section. Once you are finish with each section, be sure you click "Save"

Step C

5. Add a new section by clicking "Add New Module". Once you are done with writing your Perfect Story, go ahead and click on "Publish Story!"
